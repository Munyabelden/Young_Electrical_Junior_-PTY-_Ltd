New Electrical Installation (Domestic, Commercial, and Industrial):

We specialize in providing comprehensive electrical installation services for various settings, including residential homes, commercial establishments, and industrial facilities. Our team of qualified electricians ensures the safe and efficient installation of electrical systems tailored to meet your specific requirements.

Fault Finding (Single and Three Phase):

Our experienced technicians excel in identifying and rectifying electrical faults in both single and three-phase systems. Using advanced diagnostic tools and their expertise, they quickly locate and resolve issues, ensuring optimal performance and safety.

Solar Installation:

We offer professional solar installation services to help you harness clean and renewable energy. Our team designs and installs solar power systems tailored to your energy needs, helping you save on electricity costs while reducing your carbon footprint.

Electrical Maintenance and Repairing:

Regular maintenance is crucial to ensure the smooth operation and longevity of your electrical systems. We provide comprehensive maintenance services, including inspections, cleaning, and repairs, to keep your electrical infrastructure in top condition.

Installing Electric Motors:

We offer professional installation services for electric motors, catering to various applications and industries. Our team ensures proper wiring, configuration, and integration of electric motors, optimizing their performance and efficiency.

Air Conditioning Installation Servicing and Repairing:

Stay cool and comfortable with our air conditioning installation services. Our technicians are skilled in installing a variety of air conditioning systems, ensuring optimal cooling performance for homes, offices, and commercial spaces. To keep your air conditioning systems functioning efficiently, we offer servicing and repair solutions. Our experts perform regular maintenance, clean the units, and diagnose and repair any issues, ensuring your air conditioners operate at their best

Fridges and Cold Rooms Maintenance and Repairing, Re-gasing of Aircons and fridges:

We specialize in the maintenance and repair of refrigeration systems, including fridges and cold rooms. Our technicians conduct routine maintenance, diagnose faults, and provide effective repairs to ensure your refrigeration equipment operates reliably. Optimize cooling performance with our re-gasing service for air conditioners and refrigerators. Our skilled technicians replenish refrigerant levels, ensuring efficient operation and extended equipment lifespan for residential and commercial settings.

Attending Breakdowns and Emergencies:

We understand that electrical emergencies can occur at any time. Our 24/7 standby team is ready to respond promptly to breakdowns and emergencies, providing efficient solutions to restore electrical functionality and safety.

Intercoms Installation:

Enhance communication and security within your premises with our intercom installation services. We install intercom systems tailored to your needs, enabling convenient and secure communication between different areas of your property.

Wiring Single and Three Phase Distribution Boards:

Our electricians are proficient in wiring single and three-phase distribution boards. We ensure proper wiring connections, adequate protection, and efficient distribution of electrical power, meeting safety standards and your specific requirements.

Inspection and Testing of Electrical Installations (New and Old):

We offer comprehensive inspection and testing services for both new and existing electrical installations. Our qualified inspectors assess the safety and compliance of your electrical systems, providing detailed reports and recommendations.

Generator Installation:

If you require backup power for your premises, we provide professional generator installation services. Our team handles the complete installation process, from sizing and selection to electrical connections, ensuring reliable power supply when you need it.
